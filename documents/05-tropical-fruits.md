# Tropical Fruits: Exotic Flavors

Tropical fruits thrive in warm climates near the equator. They offer unique flavors and textures rarely found in temperate climate fruits.

## Popular Tropical Fruits

Mangoes, known as the "king of fruits," are rich in vitamins A and C. Pineapples contain bromelain, an enzyme that aids digestion. Papayas have papain, another digestive enzyme. Passion fruit offers a tart, aromatic flavor. Dragon fruit has a mild taste and striking appearance.

## Growing Conditions

These fruits require consistent warmth, high humidity, and regular rainfall to thrive.
