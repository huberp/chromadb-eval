# Bananas: The Perfect Portable Fruit

Bananas are tropical fruits that grow in clusters. They are botanically classified as berries and come from plants in the Musa genus. Bananas are one of the most consumed fruits globally.

## Nutritional Profile

Bananas are an excellent source of potassium, providing about 422 mg per medium banana. They also contain vitamin B6, vitamin C, and magnesium. The natural sugars in bananas provide quick energy, making them popular among athletes.

## Ripeness and Flavor

As bananas ripen, their starch converts to sugar, making them sweeter. Green bananas are starchy and less sweet, while fully ripe bananas with brown spots are very sweet and soft.
