# Apples: A Comprehensive Guide

Apples are one of the most popular fruits worldwide. They belong to the Rosaceae family and are scientifically known as Malus domestica. Apples come in thousands of varieties, each with unique characteristics.

## Nutritional Benefits

Apples are rich in dietary fiber, particularly pectin, which aids in digestion. They contain vitamin C, an essential antioxidant that supports the immune system. A medium-sized apple contains about 95 calories and provides 4 grams of fiber.

## Popular Varieties

Some well-known apple varieties include:
- Red Delicious: Sweet and crisp
- Granny Smith: Tart and green
- Gala: Sweet with a hint of vanilla
- Fuji: Very sweet and juicy
- Honeycrisp: Explosively crisp

## Health Benefits

Regular apple consumption has been linked to reduced risk of heart disease, diabetes, and certain cancers. The old saying "an apple a day keeps the doctor away" has scientific backing. Studies show that apple consumption can improve cardiovascular health and support weight management.
